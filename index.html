Chimera

Slow but deadly, chimeras are considered one of the most dangerous monsters. Their attacks are powerful, but they are less likely to dodge incoming attacks.

Basilisk

Hatched by a cockerel from the egg of a serpent, basilisk are known for their lethal gaze. They have the highest HP, but with medium attack strength and chance to dodge.

Griffin

Proud rulers of the skies. They are very good at avoiding enemy's attacks, but not as strong as other monsters.

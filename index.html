Curse of the necromancer

a JavaScript game

Are you ready to face most foul creatures sent by an evil necromancer? Please choose one of the beasts below so they can aid you on your journey. Each one of them is different, so choose wisely! Good luck.

Chimera

Slow but deadly, chimeras are considered one of the most dangerous monsters. Their attacks are powerful, but they are less likely to dodge incoming attacks.

Basilisk

Hatched by a cockerel from the egg of a serpent, basilisks are known for their lethal gaze. They are very resilient, but have medium attack strength and chance to dodge.

Griffin

Proud rulers of the skies, known for guarding treasures and priceless possessions. They are very good at avoiding enemy's attacks, but not as strong as other monsters.
